Huddle

Running for some is an art, and for many a personal sport, it means something different to everyone. Running is not a means-to-an-end but rather an integral part of a healthy and happy lifestyle. we want to share the joy of running with you as co-runners or friends, as well as help you in training and scaling, as your coaches.

So, those of you looking to start running for the very first time and those of you looking to break your record in the upcoming marathon, ‘Huddle’ is the bridge to join us all into one big running community, 42.1!

Meal a mile

Meal a mile is not a restaurant. Open weekends only, for now, we serve food that is sensitive to the needs of endurance athletes or anyone looking to make their day a bit healthier and a lot brighter. As part of our specially designed brunch and a la carte options, we have on offer pre and post workout meals that are vegan, gluten-free, rich in B-12 and other vitamins and minerals, loaded with good carbs and proteins or just a good cup of hot beverage. All fresh, clean and wholesome, available to eat in or on the go.

Why 42.1?

Title description, Dec 11, 2018

Pain is temporary; suffering is optional, quitting lasts forever. I never fully understood the meaning of this old saying until I started running a few years ago. For the most part of my life I have followed some form of fitness or sport. However, running simply happened to me. And what’s more - very soon in my running regimen I realized it was so much more than fitness. It was meditational, medicinal almost.
